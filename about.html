About Me

I'm Committed to exploring the ocean through engineering and science.

My Engineering Focus

I want to explore the ocean, not just visit it, but understand what’s happening beneath the surface.

What pulls me toward ocean engineering is how challenging and unpredictable the ocean is. Nothing about it is easy, and that’s exactly what excites me. I’m interested in building things that can survive underwater, move on their own, and work in places humans can’t easily reach.


I also care about what happens to our oceans in the future. I’m drawn to renewable ocean energy and technologies that help protect marine life because exploration shouldn’t come at the cost of the environment.
