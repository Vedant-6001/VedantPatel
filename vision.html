My Vision: A Better Future for Our Oceans

My goal is to innovate and deploy sustainable technologies that solve critical challenges in the marine environment, focusing equally on exploration and preservation.

My Commitment: Diving into the Unknown

When I think about my future, I imagine myself working with the ocean, not just studying it from a distance, but actually building things that operate within it. Knowing how little we’ve explored makes the ocean feel less like a mystery and more like an open invitation.

I’m interested in creating technology that can survive underwater and tell us what’s really happening below the surface. From autonomous vehicles to tough materials that handle pressure and corrosion, I want to help design systems that make ocean exploration safer and smarter.

What matters just as much to me is how we explore. I care deeply about protecting marine life and ecosystems, and I don’t want discovery to come at the cost of damage. My goal is to work in ocean engineering in a way that respects the ocean, not just uses it.

I want my work to help us understand the ocean better, while also keeping it alive for the future.
